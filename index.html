OUR PROCESS

With 3 simple steps we make your vision a reality.

FOOD SERVICE BARS

STEP 1

Once the plans are complete, our estimators get to work. They carefully review the elevations, the floor plans, the specs & measurements, to come up with an accurate quote. It’s a measured process, and we don’t cut corners, as this is often where discrepancies crop up (for example architectural vs. elevations or vs the specs), and at this early stage, it’s an easy fix. If we come across inconsistencies, we contact the client, as often as it takes, in order to ensure that we’re all on the same page, and the quote is accurate. At a later stage, we ask the client to do a walkthrough of the physical space, to make sure the equipment can be physically transported where it needs to go.

STEP 2

After the client has carefully reviewed the specs of the quote, and they send the PO, our drafting department takes over. Not only do they have years of experience, they have at their disposal the latest in 3D drafting software. This means that not only can we flesh out your equipment exactly as per the specs, we can drop in all the external food service equipment, so it’s one seamless drawing and you can clearly see if everything fits or if adjustments are necessary. Do you need to add cooling elements? heating elements? electric control panels? Does your vision include quartz, granite, millwork? We work with the best in the business for a final product that is of the highest quality, without compromising on aesthetics.

STEP 3

When the client signs off on the shop drawings, there’s usually no time to lose – it’s time for production! Our state of the art self-loading laser machine cuts the items exactly according to the specs, and the product is then whisked through the bending, notching, and polishing machines, depending on what’s needed. Although we have two levels of pricing to accommodate different sectors of the industry, the quality of every single product that our factory turns out is superb. Some of the things we do to ensure that this is the case:

  • We buy complete sheets of metal only, so we have full control over the sizing and thickness. When we tell you it’s 14 gauge, it’s exactly 14 gauge all over.
  • The tables all have round corners, rolled edges. Everything is full welded, not spot welded. As a result, our products are a dream to keep clean, with no issues like flies, leakage, mold, or grease collecting in crevices.
  • The guys who do our polishing are highly trained, and use fine, precise tools, which means we don’t lose layers of metal by overpolishing. We polish to a number four finish, which is apparent in the quality of the end product.
  • When welding, we tape around it so the finish stays immaculate.
  • The holes for plumbing and electricity are cut with the laser machine, so there’s not need to cut holes in the equipment during installation.
  • The self loading feature on our laser machine means there is minimal tampering of the stainless steel.

STEP 4

It’s now time to ship the finished product(s). We wrap each item carefully with heavy duty plastic in order to protect the finish of your final product. We have partnered with logistics companies that are known to be reliable. Once we know when the order is due to arrive, we will communicate this information to the client, and track the order until it arrives at its final destination.
